## WiFi Setup

When you first plug in your WordClock, it will enter setup mode. The text **"SET WIFI"** should be visible on the display.

**Can't see "SET WIFI"?** Long press the Mode button for 2 seconds to enter setup mode.

### Connect to Your WiFi

1. On your phone or computer, look for a WiFi network named **`WordClock-XXXXXX`**
2. Connect to it
3. Your browser should automatically open a setup page
   - If not, go to `http://192.168.4.1` in your browser
4. Enter your home WiFi name and password
5. Click **Save**

The clock will restart and connect to your WiFi. It will then automatically set the correct time from the internet.

âœ… **Setup Complete!** Your clock is now connected and showing the time.
